Hi! I am a senior undergraduate student in Software Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I work on Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (KRR) with a focus on DatalogMTL and temporal reasoning, advised by Prof. Pan Hu at the IST Lab.

My current research centers on goal-driven reasoning and incremental maintenance of temporal knowledge bases, and I am also broadly interested in applications of logical reasoning in areas such as algorithmic trading and Web3 analytics.

Research

My research focuses on Knowledge Representation and Reasoning, especially on DatalogMTL (Datalog with Metric Temporal Logic) and efficient reasoning over large temporal datasets. I am particularly interested in:

  • Goal-driven reasoning in DatalogMTL: adapting Magic Sets to temporal rules to efficiently answer complex queries over time-based knowledge bases (AAAI-25 oral, Goal-Driven Reasoning in DatalogMTL with Magic Sets).
  • Incremental maintenance of DatalogMTL materialisations: developing the first incremental update algorithm DRedMTL for DatalogMTL based on Delete/Rederive method, (AAAI-26 oral, Incremental Maintenance of DatalogMTL Materialisations).
